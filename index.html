Why most swaps fail before they start

Tron transactions need TRX for bandwidth and energy. If your wallet holds USDT but zero TRX, a typical swap can't even broadcast — you're stuck needing TRX to get TRX.

FreeTRX restructures the transaction so network costs are paid out of the swap output, not pre-funded by you. That's the whole mechanism — no subsidy, no catch.

Questions

Good to know

Do I need any TRX in my wallet first?

No. That's the specific problem this tool solves — the network fee is taken from the USDT you're swapping, not from a separate TRX balance.

Is this custodial?

No. You connect your own wallet and sign the transaction yourself. Funds move directly between your wallet and the liquidity contract — we don't hold a balance on your behalf at any point.

Which wallets are supported?

TronLink, Ledger (via Tron app), and any wallet that supports WalletConnect.

How is the rate determined?

The rate is pulled live from on-chain liquidity and refreshes every 15 seconds. The rate shown when you confirm is the rate you get, within standard slippage tolerance.

FreeTRX is a non-custodial interface for swapping assets on the Tron network. Always verify contract addresses before signing. Crypto transactions are irreversible.
